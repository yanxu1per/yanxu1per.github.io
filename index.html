Implementation

For the toaster, we used spring and designed a structure to hold and pop the phone, our design included accurate measurement to make sure the phone is completely pushed into the toaster, we used collision sensor to sense if the phone is completely pushed into the toaster, and it can trigger the step motor to hold the phone from popping put and trigger a timer. When the timer is over, the step motor will remove the obstacle and spring will help pop the phone out.

For the timer, we used a LED strip and a sigital screen to show the time passage. When time goes by, the LED strip will be lit one by one, with the number in the digital screen adding up.
